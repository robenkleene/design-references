# Photoshop 3D Frosted Glass

1. Create a new `1920x1080` file.
2. Rename the `Background` layer as `BG`
3. Choose a offwhite gray, and fill the layer with it
4. Add a `Gradient Overlay` with:
    - `Blend Mode: Multiply`
    - `Opacity: 20%`
    - White to Black
    - Toggle on `Reverse` 
    - `Style: Radial`
    - `Angle: 60`
    - `Scale: 150`
