# Premiere Pro Tasks Trim

1. Double click clip to open it in the Source Editor view
2. Use `J`, `L`, `K`, to go back, forward, pause to find the section to keep
3. Use `I`, `O` to mark in and out point
4. After in and out point are marked, use `,` to insert in timeline
5. `⌘M` to export

## Clearing In / Out Point

- `⌥I`: Clear in point 
- `⌥O`: Clear out point 
- `⌥X`: Clear in and out point

## Timeline

- `HOME` or / `END` : Move to the beginning / end of the sequence (if a clip is selected, this will go to the beginning or end of the selected clip, unselect with `⎋`)
